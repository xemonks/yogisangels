Yogi is the Creative & Managing Director of Yogi's Angels Entertainment & Decor Pvt. Ltd.

  • Education: St. Columba's School, New Delhi
  • Degree: Economics (Hons.), Hindu College – Delhi University
  • MBA: Finance & Marketing, MDI Gurgaon

He has worked with leading professionals in the European events and entertainment industry, and travelled widely across Europe, Russia, Ukraine, India and South–East Asia.
